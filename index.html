Wizards Welcome, Muggles Tolerated

More than just a tasty treat, a trip to Beau's Bonbons is like a trip to a land of fairy tales.

Featured this season–Just like in the wizarding world, we have created a shoppe you might expect to find in Hogsmead–except in the Muggle world.

Our Story

From humble beginnings, our shoppe was established in 1999 by Tonks and Dudley Longerbottom.

Transfiguring a regular candy shoppe, into a magical land–where unlike the third floor corridor–all are welcome.
